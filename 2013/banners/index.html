Retractable Banners

A set of large format, retractable banners used at info fairs and within the Campus Rec facilities to give visitors quick information about some of the programs, facilities, membership options, employment options, and ways to connect with Campus Rec. The design continues the look established by the Campus Rec guide with its bright colors and monochrome background images. All text was placed a certain distance from the ground to allow for placing the banners behind tables if needed.
