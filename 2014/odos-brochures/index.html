ODOS Brochures

A series of tri-fold brochures designed for the Office of the Dean of Students and several of its reporting units at the University of Illinois. These brochures really set the tone for the branding elements that were to be used on most marketing materials as the Officeâ€™ brand, image, publications, and digital communications were updated. Bright, colorful photos of students and campus life were specifically chosen and set off against more monochromatic areas. Note the front panel is cut a little short to expose the panel underneath which helps make the colors pop a bit and give each piece more visual interest. A vision statement and/or brief overview of what each brochure is all about has been pulled out and placed prominently as you open the brochure.
