Welcome Days is a series of programs and events offered to all new students during their first semester on campus. It is basically the last step during the admission and orientation process. The Welcome Days campaign was meant to inform new students of these important offerings and featured a postcard, poster, 16-page schedule guide, and electronic/web graphics.
