Welcome Box

New students were unable to attend the normal in-person orientation in summer 2020 due to the pandemic, so a Welcome Box and set of Illini materials were provided to all new students. The custom box included a welcome "Block I" letter, Illinois pennant, t-shirt, sticker sheet, and other Illini items.
