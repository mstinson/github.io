about me

Hello and welcome! My name is Mark Stinson. I am a designer, visual communicator, creative thinker, and problem-solver.

I am currently employed by the Office of the Dean of Students at the University of Illinois where I oversee all print and front-end web design needs for the Office and many of its reporting units.

My background includes twelve years of professional graphic design experience and ten years of web/interactive design experience. The majority of my professional employment has been within higher education settings including several positions at the University of Illinois, University of Arizona, and University of Southern Mississippi.

I received my B.S. in Design Technology – Graphic Design in 1998 from Bemidji State University. My M.A. in Communication was achieved in 2000 from the University of Southern Mississippi. I also attended a semester of classes in the Art Direction program at Brainco – The Minneapolis School of Advertising, Design, and Interactive Studies in 2002.

My passions include all things design, photography, traveling when time and money allow, Fighting Illini basketball and football, tech gadgets, and spending time with my family.

about this site

This space is primarily meant to provide a site where I can display some of my design work. It is also a place for me to try out different ideas and to continue my explorations with web and interactive design.

I fully support web standards and this custom theme has been hand-coded using valid HTML5 markup and CSS3 for layout.
